How It Works
Bring your car in

Either bring your car in or conveniently have us pick it up from you. In under an hour, our team of experts will inspect and photograph your vehicle in our state-of-the-art studio.

We prepare it

Once we’re finished, you can have your car back to drive while we list and sell it! We use the photos and inspection report of your car to generate multiple detailed listings.

We find you a buyer

Your car is marketed to thousands of potential online buyers on sites like eBay Motors, CarGurus, AutoTrader, and even Facebook and Instagram so that it quickly sells at a high value.

You get paid

We guarantee serious offers within the first 45 days of listing your vehicle. Once your vehicle sells, we pick it up from you and give you your check! We only take a flat percentage of your vehicle’s sale.
